During the **Workforce Bootcamp - First Cloud AI Journey** program, I attended two events related to AWS, Cloud Computing, Generative AI, AI Agents, DevOps, and security. These sessions helped me understand how the technologies I learned in the program are used in real projects, not only in theory or lab exercises.

The most useful part for me was that each event brought different perspectives. Some sessions focused on career direction, some explained system architecture, some included AI Agent demos, and some discussed security when deploying systems on AWS. Because of that, I could connect the event content with what I practiced during FCAJ.

### [Event 1 - AWS Vietnam Community Day 2026](4.1-event1/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** AWS Vietnam Community Day 2026

&emsp;**Time:** May 23, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** 36th Floor, Bitexco Building, No. 3 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role in the Event:** Participant

&emsp;**Main Content:** The event focused on Cloud Computing, Generative AI, Enterprise AI, GenAIOps, CloudFront, LLMs, and Multi-Agent Systems. The sessions helped me understand more about AI system design, context usage when working with language models, and how AWS supports security, performance, and system deployment.

### [Event 2 - Cloud, AI Agent, and Amazon Q on AWS](4.2-event2/)

&emsp;**Event Name:** Cloud, AI Agent, and Amazon Q on AWS

&emsp;**Time:** June 27, 2026

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Building, No. 3 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role in the Event:** Participant

&emsp;**Main Content:** The event covered practical AI Agent use cases on AWS, including Voice Agents, DevOps AI Agents, Amazon Q for human resource management, and Private Connection design for better security. The content helped me understand the connection between Cloud, AI, DevOps, and Security when building enterprise systems.

After attending these two events, I became more motivated to continue learning AWS through hands-on practice, especially in backend development, cloud deployment, observability, AI Agents, and system security.
